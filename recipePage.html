Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour: 200 grams
  • Butter, softened: 150 grams
  • Brown sugar: 150 grams
  • Eggs: 1
  • Chocolate chips: 200 grams
  • Baking powder: 2.5 grams
  • Vanilla extract: 5 grams

Preparation: 10 mins. Cooking: 10 mins. Difficulty: easy.

Steps

  1. Mix together butter, sugar and eggs untill it gets light and fluffy.
  2. Add vanilla extract, flour, baking powder and chocolate chips.
  3. Roll the dough into balls and place them on the baking sheets.
  4. Heat the oven to 180C degrees.
  5. Bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Leave to cool and serve with pleasure!
